About Us

Pinno is located near Piazza Navona and includes three charming little rooms in a mixed style of Baroque, Empire and Art Nouveau. The outdoor tables overlook the beautiful square and church Santa Maria della Pace and people from all walks of life like to stop off here at day or night to have a coffee.
